About Oliva

Built for family, rooted in hospitality.

Oliva Italian Eatery was founded by Cynthia and Justin after moving from New York City to Texas to support loved ones and create a welcoming neighborhood restaurant.

Our Journey

The move from NYC to Fort Worth shaped the spirit of Oliva: a place where every guest feels at home and every meal reflects care, consistency, and tradition.

The restaurant is dedicated to Emerson Lynn and inspired by the olive branch, representing peace, hope, and enduring family bonds.

Family Owned

A hands-on team that treats guests like part of the household.

Authentic Cuisine

Traditional Italian dishes for both date nights and family dinners.

Community Centered

A local gathering place where memories are made over food.

Our Mission

Serve with warmth. Cook with integrity. Build community.

Oliva exists to offer genuine hospitality and comforting Italian meals in a boutique neighborhood setting.

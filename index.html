Founder Story

From New York City to Fort Worth, built around family.

Cynthia and Justin relocated to Texas to support loved ones and created Oliva as a boutique eatery where hospitality leads every meal and every guest is welcomed.

Dedicated to Emerson Lynn, the olive branch represents peace, hope, and the community spirit at the center of the restaurant.
